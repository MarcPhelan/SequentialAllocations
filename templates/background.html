The Investment Game

In this study you will partake in an investment game. This game mimics important aspects of many real-world investing decisions.

You will complete the investment game multiple times. The same instructions apply to each game.

The Investment Game

Your total wealth at the outset will be ζ200. ζ is a fictional currency known as Libra.

The Investment Game

Like all real-world investments, you will have to face risk to earn a return.

Here two risky stocks (ABC and XYZ) are used.

The Investment Game

Only one stock will increase in value during each investment period.
