Payment Structure

You will complete the investment game multiple times. At the end of the study, one game will be randomly selected by the computer.

Payment Structure

If you went bankrupt during this game you will only receive the stated participation fee.

Payment Structure

If you did not go bankrupt then you will receive the stated participation fee plus some performance related payment.

In this case, one round will be randomly selected and your wealth at the end of this round will be paid to you at an exchange rate of ζ100 = £1.

Payment Structure

We will inform you which game was randomly selected at the end of the study.

Any performance related pay may take up-to 7 days to reach your account.
